Arin Joseph Hanson or Egoraptor is a Youtuber, Voice actor, and animator. He is also the co-founder/co-star of the gaming channel Game Grumps.

When Arin started his career, he started with animation in Newgrounds under the alias "Egoraptor" making comedic animations such as his "Awesome" series animations, that involved parody video games such as Pokemon.

Arin's voice has been used in multiples shows and games, such as Cartoon Network's Mighty Magiswords, Minigore, Detective Grimoire, Red vs. Blue, Rick and Morty, etc. With his voice being versatile as it is he is also in the band Starbomb. Bringing a confident personality to all of his work done so far.

Leigh Daniel Avidan, also known by his stage name Danny Sexbang, is a musician, comedian and actor. He is the co-host of gaming channel Game Grumps and part of the comedic music trio Starbomb.

In the beginning of Avidan's career he started in music by joining an indie music group called the Northern Hules in 2002. In 2009 he and Brian Wecht formed Ninja Sex Party. Later in 2013 he teamed up with Wecht and Hanson to form Starbomb.

He joined in Game Grumps by being part of the channel in 2013. In that same year he became the co-host. While in Game grumps he participated in the channel's other shows including Grumpcade, Table Flip, and Steam Rolled. Bringing a calm personlity that brings a chill environment.

In the beginning Game Grumps was created in 2012 by Arin Hanson and Jon Jafari to be a series where they played video games. Then Jafari left the channel in 2013 to focus on his own YouTube channel JonTron. After his departure, he was succeeded by Dan Avidan. Throughout time more host would join the channel such as Ross O'Donovan, Barry Kramer, Suzy Berhow, and Brian Wecht. Also the channel has had multiple guests hosts including WIERD AL, Grant Kirkhope, Dan Harmon, etc. Game Grumps have developed and published two games they created, Dream Daddy:A Dad Dating Simulator and Soviet Jump Game.

Every episode of Game Grumps usually has 2 or more hosts playing a video game, providing commentary about the game or experience with it. Also discussions occur that is either related or unrelated to the game being played, along with stories from their lives.

Game Grumps has been going for 10 years. Keeping their content mainly the same. However, they are doing more variety of content with their other channel The Grumps.
